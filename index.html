About

BlocSoc The premium blockchain group of IITR is excited to announce our first Blockchain Hackathon. We invite all interested participants to join us for an innovative weekend of hacking and building on the blockchain. The hackathion will take place on June 20 2023 and interested applicants will have to fill the form by June 10 2023.

Rules

  1. Participants must be at least 18 years old.
  2. Teams can have a maximum of 4 members.
  3. All projects must be based on blockchain technology.
  4. Projects must be completed within the allotted time that is 36 hrs
